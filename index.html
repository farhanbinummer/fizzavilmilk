What's Inside in
Fizzy AVIL MILK?

Our Avil milk is actually a concoction of mashed bananas, roasted aval or rice flakes, milk, and peanuts for an additional crunch. Roasted Avil adds to the uniqueness of the dish, lending a crunchy texture to the drink. Each ingredient added to the drink is full of vital nutrients required for the body.
